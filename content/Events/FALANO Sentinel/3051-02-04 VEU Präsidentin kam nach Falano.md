# 📅 Event: 3051-02-04 VEU Präsidentin kam nach Falano

> [!abstract] Brief Summary
> Die Kommissionspresidentin der [[VEU]] ist in [[Falano Port|Falano]] angekommen und mit ihr eine ganze Wirtschaftsdelegation. Ziel ist die Vertiefung der Kooperation zwischen Falano und der VEU bei der Besiedelung und Gründung neuer Kolonien, um neue Ressourcenfelder zu erschließen. Dabei trifft sich die Delgation mit dem Triumvirat und dem Expansions- sowie Wirtschaftsministerium

## 🕒 Timeline
- **Prelude:** Die Kooperation wurde von VEU Seite angestoßen, demnach hat das Expansionsministerium der VEU festgestellt, dass eine große Anzahl an ihren Kolonien im Grunde mehr Ressourcen benötigen, als sie selbst erstellen können. Es handelt sich also um unwirtschaftliche  Kolonien. Falano ist jene Großmacht die ein bekanntermaßen sehr lukratives Kolonialisierungskonzept erstellt hat.
- **Start Date:** - **The Climax:** Der Wirtschaftsminister der VEU deutet mögliche Zolloptionen an, die auf Güter von Falano kommend installiert werden müssten, sollte sich die finanzielle Situation der VEU nicht bessern. Sie importieren den großteil der Ausländischen Waren aus Falano.
- **End Date:** 28.01.3051 - 04.02.3051
- **Leading Figures:** [[Beatrix Sercant]], Triumviratspräsidentin Galari, [[Muhammed Liefert]], Triumviratsrichter Walaux, [[Suzanna Fari‘tak]] Expansionsministerin von Falano, [[Alec Rinnandell]], Wirtschaftsminister von Falano, [[Claudia Mandoli]], Kommissionspräsidentin VEU, [[Rudolf VanArch]], Expansionsminister VEU, [[Emmanuel Leclerc-Bauer]], Wirtschaftsminister VEU
- **Casualties / Scope:** Millions affected. Die Verhabdlungen würden weitreichende Chancen für beide Seiten eröffnen.

## 📍 Location(s)
- **Primary Site:** [[Triumviratspalast von Falanl]]
- **Secondary Sites:** [[Clubhaus der Aradan]]

## VEU President Visited Falano

A busy week for the two major nations of the VEU and Falano concludes today, February 4, 3051, with the departure of the VEU frigate AURORA NOVA.

"Such important and large delegations are always communicated to the public only afterwards, for security reasons," explains Baptiste Damiro, Commander of the Law Enforcement and responsible for the extensive security concept during the visit of the delegation from Eurom.

The proposal was accepted, and the VEU delegation was invited to Falano to initiate efforts for closer cooperation on colonization projects. "We are, without a doubt, the most successful known major power when it comes to the lucrative and charter-compliant implementation for establishing new interstellar colonies," explains Suzanna Fari‘tak, Falano's Minister of Expansion.

Digression: The Charter of the Great Bound contains detailed principles that must be observed when creating new colonies to ensure "humanity." The Charter thus addresses major problems that have arisen during colonization efforts, particularly with indigenous species.

In addition to several visits from renowned companies that wanted to host the economic delegation, negotiations were mainly conducted in the Triumvirate Palace, with the exception of one session at the Aradan Clubhouse at the request of our President Beatrix Sercant, who belongs to the house. The talks with Commission President Claudia Mandoli and between the Ministers of Expansion and Economy of both nations went well.

It was agreed to elaborate the details of this cooperation in dedicated working groups with members from both nations, so that collaboration can begin by the end of this year. However, this deadline is, from experience, more of a guideline that usually serves as a first test threshold for such high-profile international agreements. Whether cooperation will thus be possible remains to be seen.

It would mean a gain for both sides, because the VEU, given its precarious economic situation, would otherwise have to implement tariff increases, according to VEU Minister of Economy Emmanuel Leclerc-Bauer, which would particularly affect Falano as the largest exporter to the VEU territory. In addition, the joint negotiating position in the Great Bound should improve, simplifying many representation of interests.
