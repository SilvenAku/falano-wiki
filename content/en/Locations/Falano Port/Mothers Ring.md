# 📍 Location: Mothers Ring

> [!abstract] Atmosphere
> The Mothers Ring, also known as "Mom", is one of the largest circles of [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Falano Port|Falano Port]]. It is known as the worker ring — less clean than the rest of the city and somewhat overpopulated. A large number of vegetable döner stands defines the olfactory experience on its streets.

## 🌍 Geography & Climate
- **Landscape:** Sci-fi, Art Nouveau, but more elegant and somewhat plainer

## 🏙️ Districts & Points of Interest
- [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Falanomer Economy College|Falanomer Economy College]], one of the most renowned economy universities in [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Falano Port|Falano Port]]
- [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Saint-Rousseau Cemetery|Saint-Rousseau Cemetery]], a spaciously designed park cemetery with a central mausoleum and a decommissioned silicate mine serving as a burial chamber
- [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Old Rosen Cemetery|Old Rosen Cemetery]]
- [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Starbound Cemetery|Starbound Cemetery]]
- [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Sonnenmacher-Gedenkpark|Sonnenmacher-Gedenkpark]]
- (+ 2 more, smaller cemetery grounds)
- **"Foundation. Reserved."** – underground chamber at the geometric center of the hexagram; access point: barred basement window at the northwestern edge of [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Saint-Rousseau Cemetery|Saint-Rousseau Cemetery]]

## 👥 Demographics & Culture
- **Primary Inhabitants:** Workers, though it is not a dangerous place. The [[en/Factions/Falanomer Ordnungswache|Ordnungswache]] is present in the Mothers Circle as well.

## 📜 Lore & History
- **Founding:** Built in 2755-2772
- **Historical Events:** 
  - [[en/Events/3051-04-15 Das Geheimnis des Mothers Ring|3051-04-15 Das Geheimnis des Mothers Ring]] – Hexagram pattern of the six cemetery grounds discovered; underground chamber identified
  - [[en/Events/3051-04-22 Die Kammer unter dem Mothers Ring|3051-04-22 Die Kammer unter dem Mothers Ring]] – [[en/Factions/Falanomer Ordnungswache|Ordnungswache]] enters the structure for the first time in 277 years
- **Current State:** The Mothers Ring, like all areas of Falano, is under constant oversight by the [[en/Factions/Triumviratsrepublik Falano|Triumviratsrepublik Falano]]. It is simply a worker ring that appears less luxurious than the rest of the city.

## 🌑 Hidden Features 
> [!danger]- Spoiler: Secrets of the Land
> - **Underground:** Mom is home to many criminal organizations. It also hosts the [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Dark Web|Dark Web]], the largest secret marketplace for semi-legal goods. It is not illegal per se — there are very few products that cannot be traded in Falano. However, specific exceptions exist, and those can be found on the Dark Web as well.
> - **Dark Secret:** The district was planned and built by [[en/Characters/Larik Anahan Kahn|Larik Anahan Kahn]] — a co-founder of the [[en/Factions/Kigal-Shu|Kigal-Shu]]. Kahn deliberately designed the ring to form a massive ritual circle: the six largest cemetery grounds mark the exact vertices of a hexagram (deviation ø 0.3%), and at the geometric center lies an underground chamber, noted in Kahn's original plans as "Foundation. Reserved." Kahn's architectural documentation uses what he called the **Keystone Symbol** (downward-pointing triangle, crossed out) for rooms of this kind — a designation he never explained.
> - **The Chamber:** A circular room, ~12 m in diameter, vaulted ceiling, no light. At its center stands a flat stone table holding **seven sealed containers** made of an unknown material. The walls bear texts in an early form of the **Kigal-Shu-Chiffre**. Beside them, a single handwritten sheet in modern standard script: *"You have found what has been waiting. The key is not here. It will come to you."* — The chamber had been undisturbed since 2774; the [[en/Factions/Falanomer Ordnungswache|Ordnungswache]] entered it for the first time on 3051-04-22.
> - **Whereabouts of Josef Stadl-Heuriger:** The cemetery keeper of [[en/Locations/Falano Port/Saint-Rousseau Cemetery|Saint-Rousseau Cemetery]], who had identified access to the chamber, left the courtroom on 3051-04-22 after giving his testimony and has not been reachable since. His last known location: the barred basement window — which he found open the night before, despite having been sealed for at least 200 years.
> - **Unstable Elements:** Due to the ritual circle structure, the boundary between the [[en/Planes/Material Plane|Material Plane]] and the [[en/Planes/Eternal Plane|Eternal Plane]] within the Mothers Ring is considered extraordinarily thin. There are many ghost sightings.

## 📝 Notes & Trivia
- The cemeteries within the Mothers Ring find little rest — a relocation of all burial grounds is under discussion in order to reduce ghost encounters.
